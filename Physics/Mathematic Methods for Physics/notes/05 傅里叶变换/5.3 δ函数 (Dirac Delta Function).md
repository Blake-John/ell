# 5.3.1 δ函数定义

$$\delta(x)=
\begin{cases}
\infty, & x = 0\\
0, & x\neq0
\end{cases}$$

$\delta(x)$ 就是  **⌈Dirac Delta Function⌋** #def/数物方法/Fourier  ，为人为定义的 **广义函数**，满足

$$\int_{a}^{b}\delta(x)dx = 
\begin{cases}
1, & a<0<b\\
0, & \text{else}
\end{cases}$$

## 1. 挑选性

### 普通挑选

$$\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)\delta(x)dx &=\int_{-\infty}^{0^{-}}f(x)\delta(x)dx+\int_{0^{-}}^{0^{+}}f(x)\delta(x)dx+\int_{0^{+}}^{\infty}f(x)\delta(x)dx \\
&= 0 + f(0)\int_{0^{-}}^{0^{+}}\delta(x)dx + 0 \\
&= 0 + f(0)\cdot1 + 0 \\
&= f(0)
\end{align}$$

$\delta(x)$ 能够挑选出 **处于零点处的函数值**

### 特殊挑选
$$\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}f(x)\delta(x - x_0)dx&=\int_{-\infty}^{x_0^{-}}f(x)\delta(x - x_0)dx+\int_{x_0^{-}}^{x_0^{+}}f(x)\delta(x - x_0)dx+\int_{x_0^{+}}^{\infty}f(x)\delta(x - x_0)dx \\
&= 0 + \int_{x_0^{-}}^{x_0^{+}}f(x)\delta(x - x_0)dx + 0 \\
&= f(x_0)\int_{x_0^{-}}^{x_0^{+}}\delta(x - x_0)dx = f(x_0)\cdot1 \\
&= f(x_0)
\end{align}$$

$\delta(x - x_{0})$ 则能够挑选出 **处于 $x = x_0$ 处的函数值**

> [!question] 
> $\delta(x - x_0)$的定义是什么？
> 
> 令 $x - x_0 = x'$，则有 ：$$\delta(x - x_0)=\delta(x') = 
> \begin{cases}
> \infty, & x' = 0\\
> 0, & x'\neq0
> \end{cases}$$
>  即 $$\delta(x - x_0)=
> \begin{cases}
> \infty, & x = x_0\\
> 0, & x\neq x_0
> \end{cases}$$

# 5.3.2 δ函数的一些性质

## 1. 奇偶性

- $\delta(x)$ 为 **偶函数**
- $\delta'(x)$ 为 **奇函数**

## 2. 积分

$H(x)=\int_{-\infty}^{x}\delta(t)dt$ ，当$x < 0$时，$H(x)=0$；当$x>0$时，$H(x)=1$，即

$$H(x)=\int_{-\infty}^{x}\delta(t)dt = 
\begin{cases}
1, & x>0\\
0, & x<0
\end{cases}$$

$H(x)$ 称为 **⌈阶跃函数⌋** #def/数物方法/Fourier ，且有 $\delta(x)=\dfrac{dH(x)}{dt}$

## 3. 量纲

$\delta(x)$的量纲：$\dfrac{1}{[x]}$，自变量量纲的倒数 。如 $\delta(r) \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{L}$ 

# 5.3.3 δ(x)的傅里叶变换

## 1. 从δ(x)到F(w)

$$\begin{align}
F(\omega)&=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\delta(x)e^{-i\omega x}dx\\
&=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{0^{-}}\delta(x)e^{-i\omega x}dx+\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\int_{0^{-}}^{0^{+}}\delta(x)e^{-i\omega x}dx+\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\int_{0^{+}}^{\infty}\delta(x)e^{-i\omega x}dx\\
&= 0 + \dfrac{e^{0}}{2\pi}\int_{0^{-}}^{0^{+}}\delta(x)dx + 0\\
&= \dfrac{1}{2\pi}
\end{align}$$

## 2. 从F(w)到δ(x)

$$\begin{align}
\delta(x)&=\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}F(\omega)e^{i\omega x}d\omega=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}e^{i\omega x}d\omega\\
&=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\cdot\dfrac{1}{ix}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}e^{i\omega x}d(i\omega x)\\
&=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\cdot\dfrac{1}{ix}[e^{i\omega x}]_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \\
&=\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\cdot\dfrac{1}{ix}(e^{i\omega x}-e^{-i\omega x})(\omega \to \infty)
\end{align}$$

当$x = 0$时，

$$\begin{align}
\delta(x)&=\lim\limits_{\omega \to \infty}\dfrac{1}{2\pi}\cdot\dfrac{1}{ix}(e^{i\omega x}-e^{-i\omega x})\\
&=\lim\limits_{\omega \to \infty}\dfrac{1}{\pi}\cdot \dfrac{\sin(\omega x)}{x}\\
&= \infty
\end{align}$$

上式最后一个等式成立的原因是， $\sin(\omega x)$ 是个 **有界量** ，而当 $x = 0$ 时， $\dfrac{1}{\pi x} = \infty$ 。

> [!tip] 
> 上式可以视为 $\delta(x)$ 的另一种定义，任何函数可以写成这种极限形式的都可以通过 $\delta(x)$ 来表示，例如
> $$\begin{align}
> B(\omega) &= \dfrac{2A}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\infty}\sin \omega_{0}t \sin \omega t \mathrm{d}t  \\
> &= \dfrac{A}{\pi}\left[ \dfrac{\sin(\omega-\omega_{0})t}{\omega - \omega_{0}} - \dfrac{\sin(\omega + \omega_{0})t}{\omega + \omega_{0}} \right]_{0}^{\infty} \\
> &= A\lim_{ t \to \infty } \left( \dfrac{1}{\pi} \dfrac{\sin(\omega-\omega_{0})t}{\omega-\omega_{0}} - \dfrac{\sin(\omega+\omega_{0})t}{\omega + \omega_{0}} \right)  \\
> &= A\delta(\omega - \omega_{0}) - A\delta(\omega + \omega_{0})
> \end{align}$$

当$x\neq0$时，$\lim\limits_{\omega \to \infty}\delta(x)$极限不存在 ，综上

$$
\delta(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} F(\omega)e^{ i\omega x } d\omega = \begin{cases}
\infty & x = 0, \\
不存在 & x\neq 0
\end{cases} 
$$

# 5.3.4 多维δ函数

以三维为例：

$$\delta(\vec{r})=
\begin{cases}
0, & \vec{r}\neq\vec{0}\\
\infty, & \vec{r}=\vec{0}
\end{cases}$$

且满足 $\iiint_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\delta(\vec{r})d\vec{r} = 1$

### 1. 二维表示

- **直角坐标系**：$\delta(x,y)=\delta(x)\delta(y)$ 
- **极坐标系**：$\delta(r,\theta)=\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r)\delta(\theta)$ 
$\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\delta(r,\theta)r dr d\theta = 1$。
设$\delta(\vec{r}) = A(\vec{r})\delta(r)\delta(\theta)$，则
$\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}A(\vec{r})\delta(r)\delta(\theta)r dr d\theta = A(r)\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}\delta(\theta)d\theta\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}r\delta(r)dr$
$=\lim\limits_{r \to 0}A(r)\cdot1\cdot r\cdot1 = 1$，
则$A(r)=\dfrac{1}{r}$，故$\delta(r,\theta)=\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r)\delta(\theta)$ 

### 2. 三维表示

- **直角坐标系**：$\delta(x,y,z)=\delta(x)\delta(y)\delta(z)$ 
- **柱坐标系**：$\delta(r,\theta,z)=\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r)\delta(\theta)\delta(z)$ 
- **球坐标系**：$\delta(r,\theta,\varphi)=\dfrac{1}{r^{2}\sin\theta}\delta(r)\delta(\theta)\delta(\varphi)$ 

函数即为雅可比行列式的倒数，与积分时相逆 。

### 例题：计算$\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r - c)$的三重傅里叶变换，其中$r$为极径，$c$为非实数

解：

$$\begin{align}
F(k_1,k_2,k_3)&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\iiint\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r - c)e^{-i\vec{k}\cdot\vec{r}}d\vec{k} \\
&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}\int_{0}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r - c)e^{-i\vec{k}\cdot\vec{r}}\cdot r^{2}\sin\theta drd\theta d\varphi
\end{align}$$

这里需要注意的是，在球坐标$(r,\varphi,\theta)$中，$\delta(r)$是对称的，相应地，在频域$(k_1,k_2,k_3)$中，$F(k_1,k_2,k_3)$也是对称的。在球坐标系中，只要知道球表面上任意一点，则整个球面都能找到；同理，在频域中，只要知道某一个点，整个函数都能找到。

由于$|\vec{k}|=\sqrt{k_1^{2}+k_2^{2}+k_3^{2}} = k$，取$\vec{k}=(0,0,k)$。则$\vec{k}\cdot\vec{r}=0\cdot x + 0\cdot y + k\cdot z = k\cdot r\cos\theta$。

在球坐标系中$\begin{cases}x = r\sin\theta\cos\varphi\\y = r\sin\theta\sin\varphi\\z = r\cos\theta\end{cases}$

故

$$\begin{align}
F(\vec{k})&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\int_{\varphi = 0}^{2\pi}\int_{\theta = 0}^{\pi}\int_{r = 0}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{r}\delta(r - c)\cdot e^{-ikr\cos\theta}\cdot r^{2}\sin\theta drd\theta d\varphi \\
&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\int_{\varphi = 0}^{2\pi}\int_{r = 0}^{\infty}\int_{\theta = 0}^{\pi}\dfrac{1}{ki}\delta(r - c)e^{-ikr\cos\theta}d(-ikr\cos\theta)drd\varphi\\
&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\int_{\varphi = 0}^{2\pi}\int_{r = 0}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{ki}\delta(r - c)(e^{ikr}-e^{-ikr})drd\varphi \\
&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\int_{c^{-}}^{c^{+}}\delta(r - c)\dfrac{1}{ki}(e^{ikr}-e^{-ikr})dr \\ 
&=\dfrac{1}{(2\pi)^3}\cdot\dfrac{1}{ki}(e^{ikc}-e^{-ikc})
\end{align}$$
