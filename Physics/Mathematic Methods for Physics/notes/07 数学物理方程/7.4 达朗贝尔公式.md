# 7.4.1 D'Alembert Formula

我们首先研究一维弦振动的问题，其振动方程为：

$$
\dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2} - a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} = 0
$$

令 $\xi = x + a t$，$\eta = x - a t$，则有：

$$
\dfrac{\partial}{\partial t} = \dfrac{\partial \xi}{\partial t} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial \eta}{\partial t} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta} = a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} - a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}
$$

$$\begin{align}
\dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial t^2} &= \dfrac{\partial}{\partial t} \left( a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} - a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta} \right) \\
&= (a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} - a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}) (a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} - a \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}) \\
&= a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi^2} + a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \eta^2} - a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} - a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \eta \partial \xi} \\
&= a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi^2} - 2 a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} + a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \eta^2}
\end{align} \tag{7.4.1}$$

$$
\dfrac{\partial}{\partial x} = \dfrac{\partial \xi}{\partial x} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial \eta}{\partial x} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta} = \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}
$$

$$\begin{align}
\dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2} &= \dfrac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta} \right) \\
&= (\dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}) (\dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} + \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \eta}) \\
&= \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi^2} + 2 \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} + \dfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \eta^2}
\end{align}\tag{7.4.2}$$

将 Eq. 7.4.1 和 Eq. 7.4.2 代入原方程，有：

$$
a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi^2} - 2 a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} + a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \eta^2} - a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi^2} - 2 a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} - a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \eta^2} = 0
$$

可得：

$$
4 a^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} = 0
$$

因为 $a \neq 0$，则原方程化为：

$$
\dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \xi \partial \eta} = 0
$$

积分一次，有：

$$
\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial \eta} = f_2(\eta) \quad \text{且} \quad \dfrac{\partial}{\partial \xi} f_2(\eta) = 0
$$

再积分一次，有：

$$
u = \int_0^\eta f_2(\eta) d\eta + f_1(\xi) = F_2(\eta) + f_1(\xi)
$$

其中 $f_1(\xi) = u|_{\eta=0}$ 。

则：

$$
u = F_2(x - a t) + f_1(x + a t) = f_1(x + a t) + \tilde{f}_2(x - a t)
$$

若我们知道初始位置 $u|_{t=0} = \varphi(x)$ 和初始速度 $u_t|_{t=0} = \psi(x)$，则：

$$
u(x, t) = \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x + a t) + \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x - a t) + \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_{x - a t}^{x + a t} \psi(\xi) d\xi
$$

## 证明

$$
\dfrac{\partial f_1(x + a t)}{\partial t} \bigg|_{t=0} = \dfrac{\partial f_1(x + a t)}{\partial (x + a t)} \dfrac{\partial (x + a t)}{\partial t} \bigg|_{t=0} = a f_1'(x + a t) \bigg|_{t=0} = a f_1'(x)
$$

同理：

$$
\dfrac{\partial \tilde{f}_2(x - a t)}{\partial t} \bigg|_{t=0} = -a \tilde{f}_2'(x)
$$

则有

$$u|_{t=0} = f_1(x) + \tilde{f}_2(x) = \varphi(x) \tag{7.4.3}$$

$$u_t|_{t=0} = \dfrac{\partial f_1(x + a t)}{\partial t} \bigg|_{t=0} + \dfrac{\partial \tilde{f}_2(x - a t)}{\partial t} \bigg|_{t=0} = a f_1'(x) - a \tilde{f}_2'(x) = \psi(x) \tag{7.4.4}$$

Eq. 7.4.3 左右对 $x$ 求导，并乘上 $a$，有：

$$
a f_1'(x) + a \tilde{f}_2'(x) = a \varphi'(x) \tag{7.4.5}
$$

Eq. 7.4.4 + Eq 7.4.5，有：

$$
2 a f_1'(x) = a \varphi'(x) + \psi(x)
$$

解得：

$$
\begin{align}
f_1'(x) &= \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^x (a \varphi'(\xi) d\xi + \psi(\xi) d\xi) + f_1(0) \\
&= \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x) - \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(0) + \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^x \psi(\xi) d\xi + f_1(0)
\end{align}
$$

Eq. 7.4.5 - Eq. 7.4.4，有：

$$
2 a \tilde{f}_2'(x) = a \varphi'(x) - \psi(x)
$$

解得：

$$
\begin{align}
\tilde{f}_2(x) &= \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^x (a \varphi'(\xi) d\xi - \psi(\xi) d\xi) + \tilde{f}_2(0) \\
&= \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x) - \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(0) - \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^x \psi(\xi) d\xi + \tilde{f}_2(0)
\end{align}
$$

于是：

$$
\begin{align}
u(x, t) &= f_1(x + a t) + \tilde{f}_2(x - a t) \\
&= \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x + a t) - \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(0) + \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^{x + a t} \psi(\xi) d\xi + f_1(0) \\
&\qquad + \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(x - a t) - \dfrac{1}{2} \varphi(0) - \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_0^{x - a t} \psi(\xi) d\xi + \tilde{f}_2(0) \\
&= \dfrac{1}{2} [\varphi(x + a t) + \varphi(x - a t)] + \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_{x - a t}^{x + a t} \psi(\xi) d\xi \\
&\qquad + f_1(0) + \tilde{f}_2(0) - \varphi(0)
\end{align}
$$

又初始条件为 $u|_{t=0} = f_1(x) + \tilde{f}_2(x) = \varphi(x)$，则有 $\varphi(0) = f_1(0) + \tilde{f}_2(0)$。

故：

$$
u(x, t) = \dfrac{1}{2} [\varphi(x + a t) + \varphi(x - a t)] + \dfrac{1}{2 a} \int_{x - a t}^{x + a t} \psi(\xi) d\xi
$$
