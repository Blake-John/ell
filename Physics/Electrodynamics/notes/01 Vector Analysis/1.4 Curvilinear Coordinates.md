### Gradients

$$\nabla T=\frac{\partial T}{\partial r}\hat{\mathbf{r}}+\frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial T}{\partial\theta}\hat{\boldsymbol{\theta}}+\frac{1}{r\sin\theta}\frac{\partial T}{\partial\phi}\hat{\boldsymbol{\phi}}.$$
