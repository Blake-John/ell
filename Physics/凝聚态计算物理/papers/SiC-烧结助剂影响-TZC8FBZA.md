# SiC 烧结助剂影响

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22PUWPH65D%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%235fb236%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232478%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A0%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B329.1%2C275.265%2C557.69%2C282.693%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C262.984%2C557.684%2C271.761%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C250.704%2C557.691%2C258.132%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C238.423%2C342.661%2C245.851%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=1&#x26;annotation=PUWPH65D">“SiC ceramics has high room-temperature strength, but it shows a considerable reduction in strength by over 25% at 1200 ◦C due to the formation of a low-viscosity glassy phase by sintering additives in GB region”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32478</a></span>)</span>

SiC陶瓷的一个关键弱点：高温强度下降严重。

*   室温下：SiC陶瓷表现出很高的强度。
*   高温下（1200°C）：**强度会大幅下降**，降幅超过25%。

这是因为在制造SiC陶瓷时，为了提高密度，通常会添加一些**助剂**（如Al₂O₃、Y₂O₃等），称为 **烧结助剂**。这些助剂会聚集在晶界（GB）区域。在高温（1200°C）下，这些助剂会 **熔化并形成一层低粘度的玻璃相**。

这层低粘度的玻璃相起到了类似“润滑剂”的作用，使得晶界变得非常脆弱，容易发生滑动。这种晶界的弱化直接导致了材料整体强度的急剧下降。
