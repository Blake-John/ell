# 01 SiC 碳化硅的应用

## 1.1 优势

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22E9D6AQER%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232478%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A0%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B47.085%2C232.798%2C288.67%2C240.226%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C222.093%2C145.813%2C229.521%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=1&#x26;annotation=E9D6AQER">“high thermal conductivity, high electron mobility, high strength and resistance to radiation damage”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32478</a></span>)</span>

*   **高热导率**：有利于热量散发，防止器件过热。
*   **高电子迁移率**：有利于电子器件的高速运行。
*   **高强度**：保证材料在受力时的结构完整性。
*   **抗辐射损伤**：这是核工业应用的关键特性，意味着SiC在强辐射环境下能保持结构稳定，不易发生性能退化。

# 02 晶界

## 2.1 作用

### 2.1.1 益处

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22AE7U77FC%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232478%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A0%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B236.101%2C200.682%2C288.671%2C208.11%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C189.977%2C288.674%2C197.405%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C179.272%2C77.485%2C186.7%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=1&#x26;annotation=AE7U77FC">“GB can hinder the dislocation motion and thus significantly improve the strength of material”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32478</a></span>)</span>

**阻碍位错运动**，进而 **提高材料强度**。

### 2.1.2 缺点

#### 1. 热稳定性差

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22L6LQI6QK%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232478%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A0%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B221.08%2C168.567%2C288.672%2C175.995%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C157.862%2C288.673%2C165.29%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C147.157%2C288.674%2C154.585%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=1&#x26;annotation=L6LQI6QK">“he presence of GB will generate the excess energy in the system, which will make the GB structure thermodynamically unstable, resulting in the migration”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32478</a></span>)</span>

晶界导致晶体中存在 **不连续区域**， 这个区域的排列不规则， **能量较高**，因此产生了 **过剩能量 (excess energy)** ，导致这个区域的热力学性质不稳定，在高温下容易产生 **晶界迁移或消失** 。

#### 2. 晶间断裂

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232478%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A0%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B474.6830500438473%2C226.1427873%2C557.6908447251296%2C233.5709205%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C213.8617873%2C557.6900470237207%2C221.2899205%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C201.5817873%2C557.6844680971827%2C209.0099205%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C189.30078730000002%2C557.6844676189767%2C196.72892050000002%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C177.02078730000002%2C414.7431145751295%2C184.4489205%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=1">“GB is generally the mechanically weak region in materials compared with grain interiors. The local stress concentration induced by the limited motion of dislocation and GBs can result in the formation of cracks or voids near GBs, or even an intergranular fracture”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232478%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32478</a></span>)</span>

*   晶界是机械薄弱的结构：相比于内部结构紧密、排列整齐的晶粒内部，晶界处的原子 **排列比较混乱，结构不连续**，因此通常被认为是材料中 **最机械薄弱的区域**。
*   应力集中：当材料受力时，内部的位错试图移动。然而，当位错运动到晶界时，会受到阻碍而停止。这种受阻导致**能量在晶界附近积聚，产生局部应力集中**。
*   断裂机制：这种高应力会破坏晶界附近的原子键合，**导致裂纹或空洞的形成**。最终，材料会 沿着晶界发生分离，这种断裂方式被称为 **沿晶断裂**。

# 03 偏析

## 3.1 缺点

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22EPN2DNFE%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232479%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A1%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B49.543%2C730.224%2C288.674%2C737.652%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C719.785%2C288.668%2C727.213%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C709.346%2C238.951%2C716.774%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=2&#x26;annotation=EPN2DNFE">“It is of vital importance that GBs as trapping source can induce segregation of impurity atoms towards GBs [23,24], which will significantly affect the stability and strength of GBs in SiC”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32479</a></span>)</span>

晶界通常具有 **较高的能量和更开放的结构**，因此它们充当了 **陷阱** 或 **汇**。

由于这种吸引力，杂质原子（如氢、氦、氧等）倾向于从晶粒内部 **迁移并聚集到晶界处**。这种现象被称为 **偏析**。

这种聚集会极大地改变晶界的性质：

*   **稳定性**：杂质的存在可能使晶界更稳定（不易消失）或更不稳定。
*   **强度**：杂质可能削弱晶界（导致脆化）或增强晶界（起到强化作用

## 3.2 优点

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22GB9EWAKY%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232479%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A1%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B49.543%2C500.564%2C288.669%2C507.992%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C490.125%2C288.671%2C497.553%5D%2C%5B37.587%2C479.685%2C140.077%2C487.113%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=2&#x26;annotation=GB9EWAKY">“Some dopants may cause a detrimental effect on GBs, whereas others can pronouncedly enhance the thermodynamic stability and mechanical strength of GBs”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32479</a></span>)</span>

掺杂剂并非完全有害，某些掺杂剂为有益掺杂，能够 **增强晶界强度与稳定性**。

*   具体元素： 金属元素，如铍（Be）、铝（Al）、镁（Mg）和钇（Y）。
*   影响： 大多数金属掺杂原子能够 **强化晶界**，**提高其机械强度**；同时，铝和钇的掺杂能显著 **降低晶界能**，从而 **提高热力学稳定性**。
*   具体表现： 镁（Mg）原子对晶界强度的提升效果最为显著；而铝（Al）和钇（Y）在改善晶界稳定性方面表现最好。

金属掺杂会 **引起电荷重分布**，增加晶界周围的电子密度，从而增强Si-C键的强度。

## 4.2 计算目标

### 4.2.1 溶解能

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FET2T5ZZ8%22%2C%22annotationKey%22%3A%22KHNYCACF%22%2C%22color%22%3A%22%232ea8e5%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%2232479%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A1%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B318.559%2C159.783%2C557.69%2C167.211%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C149.404%2C557.687%2C156.832%5D%2C%5B306.604%2C139.025%2C375.183%2C147.616%5D%2C%5B368.004%2C136.944%2C477.451%2C147.616%5D%2C%5B469.178%2C136.944%2C531.938%2C146.453%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open/library/items/ET2T5ZZ8?page=2&#x26;annotation=KHNYCACF">“To determine the stable configuration of dopant atoms in both bulk and GB of SiC, the solution energies for an individual dopant atom at interstitial site (E_{int}^{s}) and substitutional site (E_{int}^{s}) are calculated”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F13225561%2Fitems%2FLZDIZT93%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%2232479%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/LZDIZT93">Meng 等, 2023, p. 32479</a></span>)</span> 为了确定SiC体相和晶界中掺杂原子的稳定构型，计算了单个掺杂原子在间隙位和替代位的溶解能

**Solution Energy**：这是一个衡量原子在特定位置稳定程度的指标。

*   能量**越低**：说明该位置越**稳定**，原子越喜欢待在那里。
*   能量**越高**：说明该位置**不稳定**，原子倾向于离开。

两种状态：

*   **间隙位 **- 掺杂原子挤在 SiC **晶格原子之间的空隙中**。
*   **替代位 **- 掺杂原子**替换**了SiC晶格中**原有的Si原子或C原子**。

两个位置：

*   晶界 - GB
*   体相 - bulk

通过对比计算出的 $E^{int}_{s}$ 和 $E^{sub}_{s}$ ，可以判断掺杂原子在体相和晶界中分别更喜欢哪种位置，从而 **预测其偏析行为**。

*   **纯晶界**
