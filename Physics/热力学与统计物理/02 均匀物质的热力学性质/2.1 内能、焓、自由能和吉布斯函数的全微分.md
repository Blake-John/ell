# 01 内能

将内能  $U$  作为  $S, V$  的函数，其全微分为

$$
\mathrm {d} U = \left(\frac {\partial U}{\partial S}\right) _ {V} \mathrm {d} S + \left(\frac {\partial U}{\partial V}\right) _ {S} \mathrm {d} V \tag{2.1.1}
$$
又由热力学基本方程 ![[../01 热力学的基本规律/1.14 熵和热力学基本方程#^80589d|热力学基本方程]] 可知
$$
\left(\frac {\partial U}{\partial S}\right) _ {V} = T, \quad \left(\frac {\partial U}{\partial V}\right) _ {S} = - p \tag {2.1.2}
$$
考虑到求偏导数的次序可以交换，即  $\dfrac{\partial^2U}{\partial V\partial S} = \dfrac{\partial^2U}{\partial S\partial V}$ ，对 2.1.1 求二次偏导，可得
$$
\left(\dfrac {\partial T}{\partial V}\right) _ {S} = - \left(\dfrac {\partial p}{\partial S}\right) _ {V} \tag {2.1.3}
$$

# 02 焓

焓的定义是  $H = U + pV.$  对  $H$  求微分，并将式(1.14.6)代入，得
$$
\mathrm {d} H = \mathrm{d}U + p\mathrm{d}V + V\mathrm{d}p = T \mathrm {d} S + V \mathrm {d} p \tag {2.1.4}
$$
$H$  作为  $S, p$  的函数，其全微分为

$$
\mathrm {d} H = \left(\frac {\partial H}{\partial S}\right) _ {p} \mathrm {d} S + \left(\frac {\partial H}{\partial p}\right) _ {S} \mathrm {d} p
$$
与 2.1.3 比较，有
$$
\left(\dfrac {\partial H}{\partial S}\right) _ {p} = T, \quad \left(\dfrac {\partial H}{\partial p}\right) _ {S} = V \tag {2.1.5}
$$
对上式求二次偏导，有
$$
\left(\dfrac {\partial T}{\partial p}\right) _ {S} = \left(\dfrac {\partial V}{\partial S}\right) _ {p} \tag {2.1.6}
$$

# 03 自由能

自由能的定义是  $F = U - TS.$  对  $F$  求微分，并将式 [[../01 热力学的基本规律/1.14 熵和热力学基本方程#^80589d|1.14.6]] 代入，得
$$
\mathrm {d} F = - S \mathrm {d} T - p \mathrm {d} V \tag {2.1.7}
$$

类似可得
$$
\left(\frac {\partial F}{\partial T}\right) _ {V} = - S, \quad \left(\frac {\partial F}{\partial V}\right) _ {T} = - p \tag {2.1.8}
$$
及
$$
\left(\frac {\partial S}{\partial V}\right) _ {T} = \left(\frac {\partial p}{\partial T}\right) _ {V} \tag {2.1.9}
$$

# 04 吉布斯函数

吉布斯函数的定义是  $G = U - TS + pV.$  对  $G$  求微分，并将式(2.1.1)代入，得

$$
\mathrm {d} G = - S \mathrm {d} T + V \mathrm {d} p \tag {2.1.10}
$$

类似可得

$$
\left(\frac {\partial G}{\partial T}\right) _ {p} = - S, \quad \left(\frac {\partial G}{\partial p}\right) _ {T} = V \tag {2.1.11}
$$

及

$$
\left(\frac {\partial S}{\partial p}\right) _ {T} = - \left(\frac {\partial V}{\partial T}\right) _ {p} \tag {2.1.12}
$$
