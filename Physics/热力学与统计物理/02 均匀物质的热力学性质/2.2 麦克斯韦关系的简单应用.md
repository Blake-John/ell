上节导出了  $S, T, p, V$  四个变量的偏导数之间的关系：

$$
\left(\frac {\partial T}{\partial V}\right) _ {S} = - \left(\frac {\partial p}{\partial S}\right) _ {V} \tag {2.2.1}
$$

$$
\left(\frac {\partial T}{\partial p}\right) _ {S} = \left(\frac {\partial V}{\partial S}\right) _ {p} \tag {2.2.2}
$$

$$
\left(\frac {\partial S}{\partial V}\right) _ {T} = \left(\frac {\partial p}{\partial T}\right) _ {V} \tag {2.2.3}
$$

$$
\left(\frac {\partial S}{\partial p}\right) _ {T} = - \left(\frac {\partial V}{\partial T}\right) _ {p} \tag {2.2.4}
$$

以上偏导数关系是由麦克斯韦(Maxwell)最先导出来的，称为 **麦克斯韦关系**.
